About Me

Hi, I'm Shane! I'm a Creative Specialist with over 4 years of freelance experience in design, branding, and visual storytelling. I create videos, graphics, and motion content that bring ideas to life while keeping brands consistent and engaging.

My goal is to craft visuals that not only look good but also connect with people. With expertise in modern design tools and a commitment to quality, I help bring creative visions to life through thoughtful composition and technical skill.
